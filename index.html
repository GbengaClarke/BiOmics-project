About Us

Welcome to BiOmics – A Leading Research Institution

Founded on March 13, 1999, Biomics Laboratory has led biotechnology research for over two decades, advancing molecular biology, genetics, and bioinformatics to drive innovations in healthcare, agriculture, and sustainability.

With top researchers and advanced facilities, we partner with academia, industry, and global leaders to advance bioscience, from genomics to biomolecular engineering, driving innovation in life sciences.

Join us in pioneering a healthier, more sustainable world.
